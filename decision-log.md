# Engineering Decision Log

## Decision: Keep monolith for MVP

Context:
Small team, early product.

Decision:
Use monolithic backend.

Reason:
Lower operational complexity.

Expected Outcome:
Faster delivery and easier debugging.

## Decision: Brutal honesty over user comfort

Context:
Users want clarity, not motivation.

Decision:
The system will always prioritize alignment truth, even when uncomfortable.

Reason:
Long term performance requires discipline.

Expected Outcome:
Higher trust and better results.

---

## Decision: Amateur first design

Context:
Most users have limited time.

Decision:
All recommendations will respect real-life constraints.

Reason:
Adherence is more important than theoretical perfection.

Expected Outcome:
Higher consistency and retention.

---

## Decision: Monolithic modular architecture

Context:
Early stage product with evolving domain.

Decision:
Use modular monolith with clear domain boundaries.

Reason:
Lower operational complexity and faster learning.

Expected Outcome:
Faster evolution and safer refactors.
