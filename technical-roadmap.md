# Technical Roadmap

## Phase 1 – Foundation

- Athlete profile management
- Training logging
- Competition context definition
- Basic alignment evaluation rules
- Manual rule-based feedback

Goal:
Build trust and product clarity before AI complexity.

---

## Phase 2 – Intelligence Layer

- AI-assisted training evaluation
- Context-aware recommendations
- Weekly alignment analysis
- Pattern detection in training history

Goal:
Transform data into understanding.

---

## Phase 3 – Decision Support

- "Should I train this tomorrow?" feature
- Alternative workout suggestions
- Risk and fatigue indicators

Goal:
Support real-life decisions.

---

## Phase 4 – Personalization & Scale

- Adaptive recommendation model
- Long term performance prediction
- Category and level segmentation

Goal:
Make the system feel personal, not generic.
