# Product Principles

1. Race Reality First  
Training decisions must reflect HYROX race structure.

2. Amateur-Centered Design  
Built by amateurs, for amateurs.

3. Time-Constrained Intelligence  
The product respects limited weekly training time.

4. Volume With Meaning  
Volume only matters if correctly distributed.

5. Education Over Automation  
The system explains every recommendation.

6. Brutal Honesty  
The system tells the athlete what they do not want to hear.

7. Alignment Over Motivation  
The goal is alignment, not motivation.

8. Weekly Decision Focus  
The athlete improves through weekly decisions.

9. Quality Beats Quantity  
Running quality matters more than kilometers.

10. Race Preparation Over Fitness  
This is not a fitness app. It is a race preparation system.
